# Identity & Access Scripts

Comprehensive analysis of Azure RBAC and PIM (Privileged Identity Management) assignments across your organization.

## Scripts in This Category

| Script | Purpose | Output |
|--------|---------|--------|
| [analyze-rbac](analyze-rbac/README.md) | Inventory permanent RBAC and PIM eligible assignments across management groups, subscriptions, and resource groups | CSV with role assignments + detailed statistics |

### When to Use

- **Access reviews**: Understand who has what permissions where
- **Compliance audits**: Verify role assignments align with policy
- **PIM tracking**: Identify eligible vs. active privileged roles
- **Privilege creep analysis**: Find over-privileged accounts
- **Governance reporting**: Export access landscape to stakeholders
